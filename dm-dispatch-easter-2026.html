New Initiative

Introducing: The Growth Pot 🌱

Born out of Experiment #5 (Capacity Stewardship & Unfunded Investments), the Growth Pot is a new internal investment vehicle — currently funded by JRF core funding — designed to bridge high-potential initiatives to their next phase of funding.

"Dm has organised strongly around bottom-up energy — giving ample space for teams to drive work they are passionate about. The Growth Pot adds strategic viability as a filter."

— Mission Pathways Circle, February 2026

A key feature is the Sponsor role — a strategic navigator who translates external signals into guidance for teams. Two teams are already in discovery. If you're interested in applying in future, reach out to Annette, Adam, or Eunsoo, or drop into #circle-mission-pathways.

Full overview: Notion — Overview of the JRF Growth Pot

Work in the World

Project Highlights 🌍

🏛️

Scottish Government Workshop — V&A Dundee

Adam, Georgia, Amelia, Joost, and Leah ran a full-day pop-up studio with the Scottish Government at the V&A in Dundee. The focus: what would mission-driven government actually look like in Scotland? The team drew on lessons from 100+ cities. Multiple follow-up conversations have already been requested. Adam has declared a Perthshire pub the new DML Scotland HQ.

🏙️

Sheffield Demonstrators — £800k City Goals Milestone

Sheffield City Council (via public health) signed off £800k towards the City Goals — to be channelled through the Partnership Lab. Annette and Indy were in Sheffield last week co-convening a landmark cross-sector meeting that brought together JRF, Regional Futures, Sheffield City Council, Key Fund, OPUS, and Voluntary Action Sheffield. A New Constellations event from seven years ago was cited as the original catalyst for many in the room.

🌊

Planetary Civics Arc: A Conscious Closing

Indy, Prateek, and Zehra made an announcement that's worth naming properly: the Planetary Civics Arc has achieved its goals and is formally closing as an arc. This is rare in DML history — the conscious, celebrated finishing of a phase. Projects continue (the Compendium, WAVE, Madinah, PCI Alliance), but the arc structure stands down. Kudos to everyone who shaped this body of work. 🥚

🧊

Madrid Massive Cooling Coalition — Now with Malik

Malik Lakoubay joined Dm on 24 February as Civilisation Options Fund co-lead, based in Madrid. He's also working full-time on the Madrid Massive Cooling Coalition alongside Alicia and Joost. Malik brings 15 years in market and corporate finance plus 5 years with RadicalxChange Foundation in Buenos Aires. Welcome to Dm, Malik — sports metaphors and all.

🗺️

Copenhagen Built Environment Outcome Accelerator in Innovation Atlas

The X0 (M0) Outcome Accelerator for the Built Environment in Copenhagen will be featured in Bloxhub's Innovation Atlas, launching 20 March. As Ivana noted: "Pretty cool as we haven't even started doing it yet." DML thinking, already shaping the field.

💰

Bioregional Finance: Opening "a Whole New Universe"

Leon flagged multiple soft but meaningful signals: impact investors are describing the bioregional financing publication as opening up "a whole new universe." Someone working across private investment, investment banks, and government added: "Your work has reached everyone I speak to." Soft signals, yes — but ones worth tracking.
