Key Organizational Updates

Governance Gap Committee:
First Full Month of Active Experimentation

March 2026 marked the first full month of active experimentation across the 17 GGC experiments. A comprehensive synthesis of learnings has been compiled from the 31 March learning session, Miro canvas, Notion learnings database (21 entries), and baseline survey (26 responses).

Baseline survey results show:

  • High confidence in GGC process (mean 7.6/10 across three confidence questions)
  • Significant gaps identified: Knowledge transfer (2.5/6), mission coherence (2.7/6), cross-team coordination (2.8/6), publication/authorship channels (2.7/6)
  • Strong foundation: Psychological safety scores high — comfortable admitting mistakes (5.0/6) and asking for help (5.2/6)

Project Highlights

What We're Building

Lund 2035: Evidence-Based Futures Exhibition 🇸🇪

Just launched this week! Meggan, Maria, Anette, and Alex delivered a physical exhibition at Lund City Hall exploring six radical "What if" scenarios for climate-neutral Lund by 2030.

The six scenarios:

  • What if sharing and reuse was the norm? (consumption)
  • What if the majority of public spaces were green areas? (carbon sinks)
  • What if Lund renovated instead of building new? (construction)
  • What if all children could get to school without their parents? (mobility)
  • What if all neighbourhoods were energy-positive? (energy)
  • What if all agricultural land was farmed regeneratively? (agriculture)

"Assumptions are almost always wrong until you look. I came in thinking the retrofit was cheaper than the new build. More complicated than that. Following evidence when it contradicts your frame is where the interesting work happens."

Explore the Miro board

Team: Meggan, Maria, Anette, Alex H
